BookAndBite2

A web app that pairs books with themed grocery carts — turning what you're reading into what you're cooking.

2025 · Individual project · ASP.NET Core MVC
Add cover image at
assets/img/projects/bookandbite-cover.jpg

Books often revolve around food — a dinner party in a novel, a bakery in a memoir. BookAndBite2 takes that connection and makes it practical: pick a book, and get a themed grocery cart to match its world.

ASP.NET Core MVC C#

How I built it

The app follows the standard ASP.NET Core MVC pattern — models for books and grocery items, controllers to handle the pairing logic, and views to display book details alongside their matched cart. I focused on keeping the data model flexible enough that a book could map to multiple themed carts rather than a single rigid pairing.

Challenges & what I learned

Structuring the MVC relationships cleanly took some rework once I realized my first data model didn't leave room for a book to reasonably connect to more than one cart theme.

What I learned

Getting the model relationships right early saves far more time than rushing into views and controllers. This project pushed me to actually sketch out entity relationships before writing code, a habit I've kept for projects since.
